Fanatics Worldwide

Just how far and wide across the globe does Harmony In My Head reach? I've been wondering this for some time now. This is my informal and completely unscientific attempt to answer that question. I bought a world map and colored in a few countries that I'm sure some of you fellow fanatics are from because you've said so. Now I'm asking if all of you would be kind enough to humor me and leave a comment with what country you or someone you know is listening to the show from. It can be completely anonymous, you don't have to log in or leave a name. I'll add to the list below as new ones are posted in the comments. Thanks everybody!
